About SETT Lab

Our group's research interests intersect the multidisciplinary fields of thermo-fluid sciences, surface engineering, and renewable energy. We investigate how micro/nano-scale surface structures and interfacial properties can be engineered to bring about transformational efficiency enhancements in thermal and fluid transport. With the insights gained from fundamental studies, we develop novel systems and devices for applications at the water-energy nexus. To achieve these goals, we employ state-of-the-art micro/nanofabrication techniques, visualization techniques, advanced heat transfer experimentation and numerical modeling for effective thermal transport, thermal energy conversion, atmospheric water harvesting, desalination, and thermal management of devices.
